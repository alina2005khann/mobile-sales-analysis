## 📌 Project Overview

End-to-end **3-page interactive Power BI dashboard** analyzing 
mobile sales performance across brands, cities, models and 
customer transactions in India.

**Dataset:** 19,150 units | 3,835 transactions | 20 cities | 
5 brands | 15 mobile models | 2021–2024

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 🏙️ City Performance
- **Delhi** dominates at 203M revenue (1,008 transactions)
- **Mumbai** second at 127M revenue (654 transactions)
- **Ranchi & Chennai** tied at ~31M each
- Total 20 cities analyzed across India

### 📱 Brand Performance
- **Apple** leads at 162M total sales
- **Samsung** close second at 160M
- **OnePlus** at 154M, **Vivo** at 150M, **Xiaomi** at 144M
- All brands have similar average price (~40K)

### 📅 Monthly & Yearly Trends
- **Peak months:** January (331 transactions), 
  March, April, June
- **Lowest month:** February (291 transactions)
- **Best year:** 2022 at 262M revenue
- **2024 shows declining trend** — flagged as business risk

### 💳 Payment Methods
- All 4 methods nearly equal (24–26%)
- **Credit Card** slightly leads at 26.36%
- **UPI** at 24.22%, **Cash** at 24.72%, 
  **Debit Card** at 24.69%

### 📊 Top Mobile Models
| Brand | Model | Total Sales |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Apple | iPhone SE | 59.5M |
| Apple | iPhone 12 | 50.5M |
| Samsung | Galaxy Note 20 | 56M |
| OnePlus | OnePlus Nord | 57.8M |
| Vivo | Vivo Y51 | 54.8M |
